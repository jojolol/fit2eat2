Tips for Safe Dining

  • Keep raw and cooked food separate
  • make sure you don't eat food with raw eggs
  • Keep your hands clean at all times while handling food
  • Animals are best kept away from food especially if they have been outside.
  • Your fridge should at all times be 0-5 degrees
  • Keep your kitchen worktops are always clean
  • make sure food is always thoroughly cooked through
  • make sure you always use food up by its "use-by" date
  • Make sure hot food is kept hot and cold food is kept cold
  • If you are cooking any sort of meat make sure that it is not under cooked because it can cause illness.
