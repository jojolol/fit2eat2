What is a food hygiene rating?

The food hygiene rating or inspection result given to a business reflects the standards of food hygiene found on the date of inspection or visit by the local authority. The food hygiene rating is not a guide to food quality. It appears as a certificate usually stuck to the front window of the buisness. However, they are sometimes not clearly seen or not there at all, which is where we aim to help!
